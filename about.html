Who We Are

Mali Holdings is a proudly South African and wholly black-owned black, B-BBEEE Level 1 consortium specializing in plant and logistics; agriculture and farming; power and energy; consulting services; manufacturing and engineering and automotive retail business.

he company was birthed in 2016. Mali Holdings has set a standard of excellence in delivering key services to both the private and public sectors. Mali Holdings has a regional business presence including South Africa, Malawi, Zimbabwe and eSwatini.

We strive to be among the top socio-economic development contributors wherever we operate and seek growth opportunities and partnership ventures in partnering toward this strategic intent.
